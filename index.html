2013 goals

At the beggining of the year, I set some goals that I want to share with you as a way to formalize them:

  • Improve my skills with Sublime Text 2
  • Correct the position of my fingers when typing and reach 50 WPM
  • Have weekly excersice routine and lose 6 kilograms
  • Launch Tagnfile.it and reach the first 500 users
  • Learn at least one unrelated activity like salsa dance or sailing
  • Launch new personal blog and write at least 30 posts
  • Create/Colaborate in 3 or more opensources proyects
  • Read at least 4 books

As you can see, my goals are not that hard to accomplish (well, maybe the 500 users one). The important detail about most of them is that they are measurable: 50 WPm, 6 kilograms, 500 users, 1 activity, 30 posts, 3 opensource proyects, 4 books. I did this on purpose to have some values to compare at the end of the year and then laugh at myself thinking "What in the hell was I thinking?".

Seriously, I suggest you do the same and write the goals you want to achieve this year. Do not push your limits, just do it for fun.

Welcome

I once started a blog and, like many others bloggers, ended up abandoning it. Why is this time going to be any different? Because reasons are different. Now I do it for pleasure. I received so much from the IT community during the last years that I want to start sharing things back. Maybe I can help someone around the world.

Something that I find interesting is that I have more things to share from my last 2 years as a freelancer than from my 10 years of working as a full time Software Engineer in local companies. I will share my experience in future posts for those who are interested in becoming a freelancer, but feel free to contact me if you cannot wait!

Build stuff and enjoy life!
